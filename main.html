Student Regulation: Chapter 2 – Behavior Standards

Building respect, responsibility, and faith through disciplined character!

Elyon Christian School’s behavior standards are based on its vision, mission, and values, shaping both discipline and Christian character.

Vision: Elyon becomes a progressive leader, continually committed to growth and known for its Christian values, moral, ethics, and skills as well as its ability to thrive in change.

Mission: To establish formal Christian Education with global perspective while maintaining strong Christian values and holistic education in Indonesia.

Fruit of the Spirit:

  • Love
  • Joy
  • Peace
  • Patience
  • Kindness
  • Goodness
  • Faithfulness
  • Gentleness
  • Self-control

Holistic Education (5C):

  • Christ-Centered
  • Character
  • Corpus Fitness
  • Competency
  • Creativity

Behavior Standards (Onsite Learning):

  1. Virus Prevention: Wear a mask when sick, sneeze into your arm, and bring personal hygiene tools.
  2. Greetings: Smile, greet, and show respect to everyone in the school community.
  3. Communication: Use proper English and respect others while speaking.
  4. Responsibility: Arrive early, wear a neat uniform, keep cleanliness, and submit tasks on time.
  5. Permissions: Use hall passes when leaving class and fill permission forms when leaving school grounds.
  6. Examinations: Attend exams as scheduled; missing without reason results in minimum grade.
  7. Worship Time: Show respect, take notes, and bring a printed Bible during worship.

In line with the vision, mission, and values of Elyon Christian School, students are expected to grow with discipline, respect, and strong moral foundation in both academics and faith.
